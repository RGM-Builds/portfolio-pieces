A methodology, not a tool

The Job Find Bootcamp

A step-by-step system for landing a role in 30 days, built on honest match scoring, warm outreach over volume, and materials that actually sound like you.

This is a coach, not a generator.
The goal is not just to help you get hired. It is to leave you a stronger candidate than when you started — with a clearer story, sharper instincts about what makes you unusual, and the ability to advocate for yourself in any future search. The tool educates as it executes.

Developed through a real, extended job search engagement. Every principle here was tested, corrected, and refined through actual applications, recruiter screens, and hiring manager outreach.

Before you start — recommended Claude setup

This tool works significantly better inside a Claude Project. In a standalone conversation, context disappears when the session ends. Create a "Job Search" project in Claude and run the entire bootcamp inside it — this is the single most important setup step.
Consider Claude Pro before starting
The free tier is time-gated and will interrupt the process mid-search. A 30-day job search needs sustained, uninterrupted access.
Use Projects
Create a "Job Search" project in Claude and run the entire bootcamp inside it. Every conversation in a project shares context, so Claude builds a richer picture of you over time without needing a re-briefing every session. This is the single highest-leverage setting.
Turn on Memory
Settings → Memory. When enabled, Claude automatically retains facts about you across conversations, building a richer 360 view over time without you needing to re-paste your history every session.
Turn on Web Search
Required for the batch sourcing step. Without it Claude cannot pull live roles from the web. Enable in conversation settings before starting Step 4.
Turn on Code Execution and File Creation
Allows Claude to generate downloadable resume and cover letter files rather than just pasting text. Enable in conversation settings.
Set your writing style
Upload your writing sample once under Settings → Style and Claude will reference it automatically across conversations. This means every cover letter draft will be calibrated to your voice without you needing to remind Claude each time.
1
Download and upload to Claude
Download the instruction file above and upload it to claude.ai in a new conversation. This trains Claude on the full methodology — honest match scoring, cover letter standards, outreach principles — without any personal information baked in. The system is yours to use with your own story.
claude.ai required
2
Upload your materials and build your 360 view
Upload three things: your resume, a writing sample (any piece of writing that sounds like you), and your LinkedIn profile. Claude will score how complete your 360 view is and tell you exactly what's missing. Then update your LinkedIn headline and About section before any applications go out — the right foundation before the first outreach.
360 completeness score
3
Upload job descriptions you're considering
Paste in the full JDs including the "About the Organization" section. Claude runs an honest match score against your profile using a two-part framework: can you do the actual work (60%) and do you match the stated requirements (40%). It will name gaps directly, flag hard ATS filter risks, and check your full history before scoring anything as a gap. Then verify each role is live on the company careers page before building materials.
Honest match scoring
4
Pattern recognition and batch sourcing
After three or more JDs, Claude identifies the pattern across your targets and sources a batch of 10–15 additional roles that fit your profile, prioritized by match score. You verify which ones are live. This replaces hours of manual job board searching with a focused, targeted list.
Batch sourcing
5
Build the materials — resume, cover letter, outreach
For each confirmed role, Claude builds a one-page tailored resume with ATS-beating techniques baked in, a cover letter that opens on the company not on you and sounds like your writing sample, and outreach message options to pick from. You choose the outreach angle. Claude drafts the message in your voice. Warm outreach to hiring managers is always prioritized over cold ATS applications.
Resume · Cover letter · Outreach
6
Every 4–5 applications: the audit
After every four or five applications submitted, Claude pauses the search and runs a full audit: are materials getting stronger over time, does the LinkedIn About section still match the narrative, is warm outreach happening or just cold applications, and are there roles in the pipeline that should have been skipped. It also checks in on how you're doing — job searching is hard and the pace has to be sustainable. Recommendations before resuming.
